    About The Brewery

    Le Petit Chien is a brewery that specializes in Saisons, and Farmhouse Ales. The founder and Brewmaster is Timothy Cocores who has been brewing beer actively since 2006. Timothy has travelled to different cities and countries trying local beers to create recipes to brew delicious Saisons. Some of the locations that these influences come from are San Francisco, Brooklyn, Saint Louis, Paris, Roma, and the small town in Central Mexico; Aguacalientes. By developing recipes from these different locations we were able to create the Classic Saison and paint it in a different light, making flavors that are delicious and destinctive to a specific region.

    Our Values...

    Here at Le Petit Chien, we have strong values. We believe that a beer should be made with love, instead of being mass produced on an assembly line. Each of our beers are made small batch with locally sourced ingridents. We use experimental techniques on our beers, which each beer is tried and tasted by the employees at the company before it leaves our Brew House. We take pride in a product with the belief if we would not drink it then why should you?
